Outer Wilds is an open world mystery about a solar system trapped in an endless time loop. As a new recruit of the Outer Wilds Ventures, you set out to explore your home planet, Timber Hearth, and the rest of the solar system. But be careful: the time loop resets every 22 minutes, and if you die, you wake up back at the start of the loop with no memory of what happened before. Can you uncover the secrets of the ancient Nomai civilization and save the solar system from destruction?

The Planets in Outer Wilds all pose different challenges and mysteries. Every celestial body is vital to understanding the plot of the game, and exploring each and every one of them is the real magic behind the game.
